Donovan Paul Yeuell III

1951 - December 20, 2022

Biography

Paul came from a military lineage — his father, Col. Donovan Paul Yeuell Jr. (USMA 1940), is buried at West Point, and his grandfather Col. Donovan Paul Yeuell Sr. (1894–1953) preceded them both. Paul broke from that path and graduated from Stanford in 1974 with a B.A. in American Literature, where he was co-captain of the lacrosse team. He spent many years in Los Angeles as a working actor and writer. He returned to UCLA Anderson and earned his MBA in 1996. In 2018, at age 67, he reinvented himself again, moving to Mount Vernon, Washington in the Skagit Valley to build a commercial heirloom tomato business. He was an ocean swimmer and paddler, a mountaineer, and devoted to his Belgian Tervuren, Harley.
