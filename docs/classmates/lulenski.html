Jeffrey Steven Lulenski

1967 - April 24, 2020

Biography

Jeff was raised in St. Joseph, Michigan, the son of Dr. Gary C. Lulenski. He graduated from Michigan State University in 1990, then joined SAS Institute as an account representative based in Chicago. He met his wife Heather and later moved to California, ultimately settling in San Jose. After earning his UCLA Anderson MBA in 1996, Jeff built a 25-year career in business development across media and digital — at Deloitte, Tribune, Knight Ridder Digital, Yahoo!, Manilla, hibu, and GoDaddy.

Obituary

Jeffrey Steven "Jeff" Lulenski, of San Jose, California, died on April 24, 2020, at age 52. A graduate of Michigan State University (B.A. 1990) and the UCLA Anderson School of Management (M.B.A. 1996), Jeff spent his career building business-development and partnership organizations across the technology and media industries — at Deloitte, Tribune, Knight Ridder Digital, Yahoo!, Manilla, hibu, and GoDaddy. He was a music and film lover and a sports enthusiast. He is survived by his wife Heather and daughters Sienna and Kelsey, and by his brothers Michael and David. His stepmother Kathryn ('Kaye') Lulenski passed in 2022; his father Dr. Gary C. Lulenski passed in 2025. Friends and colleagues remember Jeff for his easygoing humor, his loyalty, and his quiet competence — the kind of partner who made every team and every deal he touched better.
