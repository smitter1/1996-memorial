Stefanie L. Verkauf

April 9, 1967 - December 4, 2010

Biography

Stefanie was born April 9, 1967 in Baltimore and raised in Tampa, the daughter of Dr. Barry S. Verkauf — a pioneering Tampa reproductive endocrinologist who performed Florida's first successful IVF birth — and Arline Verkauf. She graduated from Berkeley Preparatory School in Tampa, then earned a B.A. in Theater Arts from the University of Pennsylvania in 1989. Before UCLA Anderson, she was Associate Director of Development at Playwrights Horizons in New York. After earning her MBA in 1996, Stefanie applied her arts-and-fundraising background in San Francisco, working in development at the San Francisco Ballet and the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art. She later married Michael Costello and the family moved to Miami.

Obituary

Stefanie was remembered as creative and always interested in the performing and visual arts and their value to society. The Tampa Bay Times described her life as 'a life half finished.' She worked for the San Francisco Ballet and the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art before relocating to Miami after marrying Michael Costello. The family asked that donations be made in her memory to Voices Against Brain Cancer.

Memorial Legacy

UCLA Anderson maintains the Stefanie L. Verkauf Memorial Fellowship, supporting MBA students in her memory.
